ICO

We intend to issue 150million poker tokens to the public in the first round. We anticipate to distribute tokens to early token buyers at a deep discount. .10 cents or less in the first round of ico.

Token structure:
200million PTs to be held as float for players who play in other currencies such as bitcoin, ethereum, and other currencies.

200million token will be issued as awards, rake loyalties to white labels and licensee holders, and special premium for prop players, contests, bonuses, and extraordinary award for founders and employees.

250milion tokens are held in reserve for growth marketing measure, which will be released only through governance voting and fixed conditions.

Each token holder will be able to:

  • Play on the token tables and tournaments
  • Purchase a whitelabel to launch their own poker room
  • Be part of the licensing quorum, by issuance of whitelabels.
  • Vote via our governance system on community issues and directions.

TECHNOLOGY

The network will be powered by the Poker Token, a crypto token based on Ethereum (ERC 20). This token will govern the network by means of using advanced cryptography, cryptocurrencies, smart contracts, and blockchain technology

Blockchain

The blockchain data allows for transparency and verification of correct results and payments. The blockchain would also publish information on players (non personal details) accessible for external people to verify the integrity of the game, anomalies, security issues, and ever derive statistical research data.

Smart Contracts

A smart contract is a digitized protocol meant to facilitate, ensure, and verify the negotiation and or performance of a contractual agreement, without the need of a middle man.

Homomorphic Encryption

Homomorphic encryption can be used to securely chain together a variety of commands without exposing sensitive data, this form of encryption can also be used to establish secure voting systems, and anti collusive hashing functions within the network.

What is the Poker Token Network?

The PTN is a network where we plan to launch a Poker Game through licensing and white labeling features available to anyone who wants to run their own poker room.

We have network concepts where our licensees and players can participate in massive tournaments with extreme large prizes.

Reasons to choose the poker token network

Poker Token

The token is ERC 20 based. Smart contracts, and safe and secure blockchain cryptographic solutions will be deployed to have the most secure, and fair poker games.

Trusless Systems

We use Blockchain, Smart contracts and Homomorphic encryption to ensure the safest of game environments with no central server that generates cards.

Anticollusion

Blocks in our blockchain would be produced through player activity, as sort of proof of participation model.
